Our Story

A different way
to live with art.

Atelier began with a simple frustration — that buying meaningful art online felt either intimidating or impersonal. We started small, with a handful of artists we admired, and built the marketplace we wished existed.

Our Promise

Hand-picked. Always.

Every artist on Atelier is reviewed by our curators. We're not interested in being the biggest marketplace — we want to be the one you trust when you want something real.

From the artist's brush to your wall, we make sure each piece arrives the way it was intended to be seen.

Our Mission

Artists First.

The art world has historically taken too much from the people who make the work. We're flipping that. Artists keep up to 70% of every sale on Atelier — among the highest rates anywhere.

When you buy from us, you're not just buying a print. You're funding the next painting.

Want to be part of it?

Whether you collect or create, there's a place for you at Atelier.
